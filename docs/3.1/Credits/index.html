Aegisub was originally written by Rodrigo Braz Monteiro with significant contributions from Niels Martin Hansen. Development was lead by Monteiro through 2008, Hansen through 2010, and by Thomas Goyne from 2011 onward.

Programming

  • Main Programmers:
    • Rodrigo Braz Monteiro (ArchMage ZeratuL) - Lead developer, video system, audio system, subtitles I/O, tools
    • Niels Martin Hansen (jfs) - Automation, Lua automation, audio system, tools
    • Thomas Goyne (Plorkyeran) - Lead developer for 3.0 onwards.
  • Staff Contributors:
